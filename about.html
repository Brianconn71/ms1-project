The story of manson avenue

Who we are?

We are Manson Avenue. We are an indie rock band based in the west of Ireland. We were formed on a schoolyard playground in 2012. We sing our own original tracks and we cover some more popular songs at events and functions. We have been told we do a mean Mr. Brightside (but who doesn't!). Our original songs include "I'm not a boy, not yet a man" and "Everything is decent"

Where did it begin?

Our journey began in a schoolyard in a small village in Sligo called Kiltubber. Larry wanted to enter a christmas talent show, but didn't want to do it alone so he asked his pals Timmy and Reginald to form a band. The called themselves Eastlife but found the marketing and branding to be a difficult task and louis Walsh was said to be displeased. So, they put two random, moody words together and came up with Manson Avenue and since 2012 they have gone from strength to strength.

What do we do?

We make music. We write and produce our own songs and we play at functions and events. We always play what the crowd likes and never fail to fill a dance floor. Once Timmy gets a few in him he is a sight to behold and usually the first man to lead a conga or suggest going for a streak!
